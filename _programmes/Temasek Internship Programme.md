<h2>TEMASEK INTERNSHIP PROGRAMME</h2>
<p>The <strong>Temasek Internship Programme (TIP)</strong> is an intentional
bridge between your current studies and your future career. Targeted at
JC1 students, this mid-year attachment focuses on <strong>career exploration.</strong>
</p>
<p>By spending two weeks with our industry partners, you will gain a realistic
perspective on the professional world. The programme emphasizes growth
through:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Direct Experience:</strong> Understanding the true pace and demands
of an industry.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Active Reflection:</strong> Using a personal journal to track your
daily learning and a final reflection to synthesize your takeaways.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Our Intent:</strong> We want you to return to school with a clearer
sense of purpose, a better understanding of your career goals, and the
confidence to navigate your future path.</p>
<p></p>
<h4>WHO ARE OUR PARTNERS?</h4>
<p>The mentors and agencies involved represent a good spectrum of wide-ranging
specialities including architecture, bio-technology, community, infocomm
tech, law, performing arts, animal care, medical, digital marketing, and
many others.</p>
<p>2 to 3 students will serve as interns in each of the agency. A rigorous
selection process will ensure good matching between interns and mentor/agency
in order to achieve synergy in this programme.</p>
