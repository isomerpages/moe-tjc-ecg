<p>Curious about what happens behind the scenes of your favorite industries? <strong>TIP Lite</strong> offers
a series of Education and Career Guidance (ECG) learning journeys designed
to take you beyond the classroom and into the heart of the professional
world. Whether it’s a site visit to a cutting-edge office or an insider
career talk, TIP Lite gives you a "day in the life" look at different sectors.
Don’t just wonder what a career looks like—come see it for yourself and
start making informed steps toward your future.</p>
<p>In 2025, our students stepped beyond the classroom and into the real world
– exploring diverse industries and uncovering exciting career possibilities.
From renewable energy at the Sungei Tengeh Floating Solar Farm, to financial
literacy at Money Mindfulness, AI Innovation at Fabrica AI, and cutting-edge
research at the NUS Centre for Hydrogen Innovations and the Sustainable
Tropical Data Centre Testbed – every visit offered a powerful glimpse into
the future of work.</p>
