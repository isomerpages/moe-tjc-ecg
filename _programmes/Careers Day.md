<h4>Careers Day</h4>
<p>The Careers Day is a special day whereby students will explore the world
of work, self, future and aspirations. Tailored to the Year 4 Students,
the day of exploration includes:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Understanding the changing landscape of work in Singapore,</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sharing by Year 5 students of their internship experiences during Wonder.Observe.Weave!
(W.O.W!) Programme</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Careers, Scholarship and Higher Education (CSHE) Fair</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Activities that enable in-depth exploration of the world of work (e.g.
Career Simulation Game, etc.)</p>
</li>
</ul>
